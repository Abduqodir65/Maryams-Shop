Introduction

This is a wonderful pilaf recipe passed down through generations. You can make it very easily and itâ€™s absolutely delicious!

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount Note
Rice 1 kg Devzira or Lazer
Meat 500 gr Lamb or beef
Carrots 500 gr Cut into thin strips
Onion 3-4 pcs Medium-sized
Oil 200 gr Vegetable or lamb fat

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat oil in a cauldron
  2. Fry the onion
  3. Add the meat and fry
  4. Add the carrots and fry
  5. Add rice, pour water over it
  6. Simmer until ready

Cooking Tips

  • Soak rice for 2 hours
  • Cut meat into large pieces
  • Cut carrots into rural style
  • Use spices moderately
